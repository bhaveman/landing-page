Taverns, Tales and Tastings

May 31st and June 1st, Edmonton

Event Details

Imagine yourself seated in a cozy tavern, surrounded by fellow adventurers eager to embark on a journey of epic proportions. As you sip on your ale and feast on hearty fare, you'll be transported to a world of imagination, where every decision you make shapes the course of your destiny.

With our expert game masters guiding the way, you'll delve into dungeons, battle fearsome creatures, and uncover hidden treasures - all while enjoying a sumptuous tavern-style dinner that's fit for a hero. So, gather your party, sharpen your wits, and prepare for an unforgettable evening of adventure, laughter and delicious delights. The quest awaits - will you answer the call?
